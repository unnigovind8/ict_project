Election

Kerala, known for its vibrant political scene and active voter participation, provides an interesting background for electoral analysis. With a rich history of democratic governance, Kerala's general elections serve as a representative of India's diverse political landscape. In this project, we delve into the Kerala general election history dataset to unravel the complexities of electoral dynamics and predict candidate positions in subsequent elections. By utilizing the power of data science techniques and machine learning algorithms, we seek to uncover hidden patterns and trends within the dataset. Our ultimate goal is to develop a robust classification model that can accurately predict the election outcome based on numerous factors, ranging from demographic characteristics to campaign strategies. In addition to root about the Kerala general election history dataset to predict outcomes, our project extends its reach to democratize access to electoral insights through a user-friendly web application. By integrating data science techniques with web development, we aim to bridge the gap between electoral analysis and public engagement. By combining the power of machine learning algorithms with web development technologies, we endeavor to empower users with the tools and knowledge necessary to navigate the complexities of electoral processes. Through the hosting of our predictive model on a website, we aim to catalyze informed discussions and promote civic engagement in Kerala's electoral affairs.

Knowledge Center

Voters

Once the draft electoral roll for a Local Self Government Institution is published, the claims and objections, if any, thereon have to be preferred before the Electoral Registration Officer (ERO).

Preparation of Electoral Rolls

The electoral rolls for all the constituencies comprised in a Village Panchayat shall be prepared and revised by an electoral registration officer who shall be such officer of the Government or of a local authority as the State Election Commission may, in consultation with the Government, designate or nominate in this behalf.

Election Officials

District Collectors have been designated as District Election Officers in all the 14 districts in the State. There is a Deputy Collector (Election) and an Assistant each in district to assist the District Election Officer.
